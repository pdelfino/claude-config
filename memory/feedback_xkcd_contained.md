On PR le-day-club#473 (2026-06-11) Pedro rejected spreading the hand-drawn/XKCD look into UI chrome: "I think this PR actually looks ugly. The XKCD influence needs to be more self contained." He had everything bold stripped: wobble sketch borders on cards, paper grain overlay, the sun logo draw-in animation, AND the terracotta full-bleed contact band. He kept the Alegreya serif headings and the staggered hero reveal.

**Why:** The XKCD sketches are an illustration voice, not a site-wide texture. Surfaces (cards, borders, backgrounds, sections) should stay clean and restrained; personality lives in the illustrations and the copy. Big full-bleed color moments also read as too much for this brand.

**How to apply:** When doing UI work on le-day-club, keep crisp borders, flat clean backgrounds, no texture overlays, no animated logo gimmicks. Refined typography and one well-orchestrated entrance animation are the approved level of boldness. Propose anything louder as a screenshot/option first, not as shipped code. See also [[feedback-no-em-dashes]] for his general anti-flourish taste.
