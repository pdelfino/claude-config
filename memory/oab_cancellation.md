Goal: stop anuidade charges since he lives in Canada and works in tech, not law. His carteira "numero de seguranca" is 16045401 (the form asks for it alongside numero de inscricao). Seccional/state not yet recorded; ask once and update here.

Justificativa text he used (drafted together): cancelamento a pedido, art. 11, I, Lei 8.906/94, permanent residence abroad, request pro-rata anuidade for immediate settlement.

Road back, settled before he filed: NO new Exame de Ordem ever needed (approval never expires); re-entry = nova inscricao meeting art. 8 requirements (diploma, certidoes, idoneidade, no incompatible job), new oath before the Conselho (traditionally in person at the seccional), new fees, and a NEW inscription number (old one unrecoverable). No deadline or penalty to return.
